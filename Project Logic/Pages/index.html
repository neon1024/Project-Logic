This Site Is Dedicated To The Artist Logic.

An American Retired Rapper, Songwriter, And Record Producer, As Well As a Streamer And Author.

As a rapper, Logic released six studio albums and received two Grammy nominations.

Logic cites Frank Sinatra as his main inspiration. Sinatra's influence can be seen in several aspects of Logic's persona
